About KwickEat

In today's fast-paced campus life, long queues and delays at popular food spots often lead to wasted time and reduced productivity for students. Recognizing this challenge, our team designed KwickEat — a smart pre-ordering platform that allows students to place orders in advance, make quick digital payments, and collect their food using a unique PIN — eliminating the need to wait. This solution enhances convenience and optimizes order management for shopkeepers, creating a seamless bridge between efficiency and satisfaction.
